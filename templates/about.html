About Flea Market

The Flea Market Product Information System is a full-featured Web-based demonstration application for the creation and maintenance of an online marketplace where users can buy and sell second-hand items. This application is implemented using Django.

Flea Market allows users to:

  • Browse products
  • Search for specific items
  • Add items to shopping cart
  • Complete purchases
  • Track order history
  • Sell their own items

To access all features, please login or register for an account.
